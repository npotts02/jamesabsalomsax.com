About Me

James Absalom BA, Leeds Conservatoire

James studied tenor saxophone at Leeds Conservatoire and achieved a BA Music degree with a first. He has centered himself in the heart of the Leeds Jazz scene, playing in iconic venues such as: Sela Bar, The Domino and Brudenell Social Club. James is keen for any playing with his heart lying in the Jazz genre.
